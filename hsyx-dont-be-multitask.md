# Don't be Multitask
#tasks #myth #howtodo #efficiency

- Do not be multitasks, it is important to focus on only one thing.
- It is a myth that humans can focus on one more things at time.
- Multitasking drains your energy and cause more fatigue. Usually people think they are more productivity in
  multitasking however it is a myth
- Diminishes the number of decisions, because more decisions make you more tired during tasks
- Have a structure and system for everything and apply the same steps ever.
