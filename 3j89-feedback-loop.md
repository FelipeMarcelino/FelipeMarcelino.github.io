# Feedback Loop
#selfimprovement #feedback #method

Feedback loop is a way to receive the feedback after something happens. This feedback can be positive or negative, it does
not matter, the important here is to have the feedback, return to the activity and rerun the feedback again.
Some points are important to address:
- Try to not give a huge feedback in the final with all the points you have to address. It is better to have small steps
  regarding the feedback, so that is why feedback loop is important. Receive feedback, go to activity, then receive
  feedback again.
- The feedback is about of the things ==someone is doing and about not about him/her/they==.
