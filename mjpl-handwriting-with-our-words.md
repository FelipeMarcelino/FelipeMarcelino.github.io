# Handwriting with our words
#writing #notes #memorize #ideas #reading #learning

- Write a text in our language, [[ndec-literature-notes]] and [[wz0b-permanent-notes]], is a way to fit something from
  other context into our own context inside the [[8imv-slip-box]]
- The idea you are writing can have connections, so try to write it thinking with connections this knowledge/idea can
  be connected.
	* Are these ideas contradictory? They complement each other? These notes are question and answer? How these notes
	  help me to explain specifics phenomenons? What does that mean?
- While starting a topic [[wz0b-permanent-notes]] can be quite extensively because we want to capture big pieces of
  context on the notes. In that way our notes will be enough information when you read it later.
- Handwriting notes are better to learning because they are slow to be done and, we need to understand a topic before
  writing about it. These notes need to be assertive.
