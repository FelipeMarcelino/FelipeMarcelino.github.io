# Outline
#notes #system #writing #zettelkasten #organization


- Outline is focused on the structure of the text, does not focus on the text itself.
- Combine notes between itself (connections) is another tasks not related to outline.
- The outline combine multiple sources (Multiple [[wz0b-permanent-notes]]#) to create a paper or
  full complete ideas or texts
- Try to use the connections between notes to understand about a specific topic and sort the outline with logic reason
  behind notes
- Transcript the outline to create a manuscript
- Proofread the manuscript
