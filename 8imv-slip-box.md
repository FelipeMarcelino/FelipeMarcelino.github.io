# Slip-Box
#zettelkasten #ideas #writing #organization

- Slip-box is place where you put your notes and organize them by index
- The idea to have a Slip-box is to change you habits to take notes
- Use the method #[[38lz-zettelkasten]] to understand about multiple topics and make any read not wasted of time creating multiple
  notes
- Slip-Box is the place where we put our mental models, and our connections between our knowledge.
- Try to think where new notes can be connected in the Slip-Box
- It will help to develop ideas [[h5ky-develop-ideas]]#
- Slip-box are compound of multiple [[wz0b-permanent-notes]]#
- You never will have a overview of your Slip-box
