# Zeigarnik Effect
#psychology #mind #tasks

- It states that people remember incomplete, unfinished, interrupted tasks better than completed ones.
- A way to clear our minds is related to Zeigarnik Effect. We got easily distracted by unfinished tasks. Complete
  this tasks to clear our minds.
- Other way to things is that usually we solve our problems when we are doing errands, or without exactly think on the
  problem we are trying to solve.
