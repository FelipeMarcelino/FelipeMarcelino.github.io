# Develop Ideas
#zettelkasten #notes #organization #ideas

- Ideas are developed using a combination of [[wz0b-permanent-notes]]
- Ideas need to be mingled or mixed to create new ideas or new concepts
- Give small steps into a direction
- Try to focus on small differences on concepts almost similar.
- Try to open multiple notes to make the thinking more narrow
- Codify the information you want to remember: [[11u2-how-to-retrieve-information]]#
- Always compare the notes, the addition of a new note can make you **fix**, **complement** or **improve** your old
  notes.
- Think independently is hard but going to be easy if you have enough elaboration (linked notes) about a topic you want
  to talk
- Important questions need to be done [[zkr2-question-about-a-topic]]# and they can change through the time
- Always develop questions like: What if?
- Try to check if your note is really what your understood and make sense with the text
- Find the holes on your ideas and try to find notes to fill them. Verify what is not on them notes but can be
  considered relevant
- If we understand something well we know the limitation (Fill the holes, make connections)
- [[nx1q-project-notes]]# can be a combination of multiple ideas and realized as a paper, manuscript, book, blog post, a software project.
