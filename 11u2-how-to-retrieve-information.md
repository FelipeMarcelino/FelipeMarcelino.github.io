# How To Retrieve Information
#zettelkasten #connection #method #howtodo #organization #notes

- Codify the information you want to maintain including clues to it
- Practice to remember
- Try to remember about notes between time or when you are trying to make new connections using [[wz0b-permanent-notes]]
- Much more connections = more contexts to retrieve the information you want to remember
- "Which situation I want to remember it?" Make this question about each note
- In the beginning the connection is scarcity, although after some time will be the first thing you will do on your [[8imv-slip-box]]
- The index need to have only entries to other topics
- Only way to retrieve information are the use of tags: [[dz2g-how-to-use-tags-in-zettelkasten]]#
- Tags need to be related to topics not to note itself.
